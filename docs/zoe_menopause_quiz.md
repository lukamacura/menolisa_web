## Part 1: Basic Demographics & Medical History
The quiz begins by collecting baseline data to personalize the user's results.

### 1. Let's start with the basics
*   **Your age:** Numerical input (in years).
*   **Your height:** Toggle available for feet (ft) or centimeters (cm).
*   **Your weight:** Toggle available for kilograms (kg), stones (st), or pounds (lbs).

### 2. Menopausal Status & Hormone Therapy
**What is your menopausal status?** (Options provided with brief definitions)
*   **Pre-menopausal:** (have not started menopause)
*   **Perimenopausal:** (periods may be irregular, or experiencing symptoms like hot flushes, mood changes, sleep disturbances, brain fog)
*   **Post-menopausal:** (periods have completely stopped, at least 1 year ago)
*   **I'm not sure**
*   **I'd rather not say**

**Have you ever, or are you currently, taking any forms of menopausal hormonal treatment (HRT)?**
*   I am currently taking HRT
*   I have taken HRT in the past
*   I have never taken HRT

## Part 2: Symptom Assessment
The core of the quiz asks the user to rate how much they have been bothered by specific menopause symptoms in the past week.

**Severity Scale (used for all symptoms):**
1.  Not at all
2.  A little
3.  Quite a bit
4.  Extremely

### Evaluated Symptoms:

| Category | Symptoms |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Vasomotor & Physical** | Hot flashes, Night sweats, Palpitations, Joint and muscular discomfort, Weight gain |
| **Sexual & Pelvic Health** | Loss of interest in sex, Bladder problems, Vaginal dryness |
| **Appearance / Dermatological** | Declining skin quality, Declining hair quality |
| **Mood & Cognitive** | Irritability, Low mood/depression/mood swings, Anxiety, Brain fog, Memory problems |
| **General Wellbeing** | Sleep problems, Headaches, Tiredness and fatigue, Decreased physical strength, Digestive symptoms |

## Part 3: Data Capture
> "Almost there..."

Before revealing the results, the platform uses an email capture gate. The user must enter their email address to receive:
*   Their personalized **"MenoScale"** score.
*   A comparison of their symptoms to other women.
*   A **Menopause Nutrition Guide**.
*   A one-off explainer about ZOE.

*(**Disclaimer:** Notes that they will also receive ongoing science and nutrition emails, discounts, and offers).*

## Part 4: Results & Analysis
Once the email is submitted, the user is presented with their customized report based on their inputs.

### 1. The MenoScale Score
*   **The User's Score:** In this example, the user received a score of **50**.
*   **Headline:** "Your score is higher than average"
*   **Visual Benchmark:** A horizontal bar chart visualizes the score, highlighting where the user sits on a scale relative to Perimenopausal and Postmenopausal benchmarks.
*   **Contextual Data:** The results compare the user to a specific demographic cohort (women aged 41-45).
    *   **Average score for perimenopausal women (aged 41-45):** 31
    *   **Average score for postmenopausal women (aged 41-45):** 32

### 2. Symptom Comparison ("You're not alone")
This section isolates the user's top three most severe symptoms and compares them visually against the "Average for similar women."

*   **Legend:** 
    *   **Yellow bar:** Represents "You" (the user)
    *   **Dark Red bar:** Represents the "Average for similar women"

**The User's 3 Main Symptoms (Example Case):**
*   **Irritability:** User (Yellow) is nearly at the maximum; Average (Red) is ~50-60%.
*   **Tiredness and fatigue:** User is maxed out; Average is notably lower.
*   **Joint and muscular discomfort:** User is around 60%; Average sits at roughly 25%.
