## 🛠️ Funnel Implementation Map: Stage-by-Stage

### 1 - Cold Ad

- **Her Rung (Entry):** Grief / Fear *(lost herself, anxious, dismissed)*
- **The One Job:** Lift her one rung up to **Courage** *(the action step)*. Enter on the heavy emotion using your Angles page formulas (Problem–Solution, Curiosity, Social Proof, or careful Fear) and transition to hope.
- **The One Ask:** The click. Close on *"get your 2-minute score."* Nothing else.

### 2 - Quiz Click → Quiz

- **Her Rung (Entry):** Moving above the line into **Motion**
- **The One Job:** Build momentum toward **Willingness**.
- **The One Ask:** Exactly one tiny ask per screen.
- **Implementation Note:** Keep the Goals question (Question #4) right before Reward 1 to lift her into *Desire* before the first dopamine hit.

> ⚠️ **Critical Hypothesis Rule:** The Goals question is the thread you pull at the very end. Capture her desired outcome in her own words (or a fixed pick). **Do not paraphrase it later - repeat it verbatim** at the results page (*"what you get back"*) and at the paywall (*"{her goal} in 8 weeks"*). This verbatim echo is the exact mechanism that returns her to where she started.
> 

### 3 - Calculating Screen

- **Her Rung (Entry):** Holds her at **Willingness**
- **The One Job:** Increase psychological investment through an open loop and anticipation.
- **The One Ask:** Wait for processing.
- **Optimization:** Add copy: *"Comparing your answers to thousands of women like you"* to seed authority and belonging before she sees the score.

### 4 - Email Gate (Magic Link)

- **Her Rung (Entry):** High risk of dropping back down due to skepticism *(avatar filter: "is this a scam?")*
- **The One Job:** Reframe the friction from a toll into a benefit to prevent a spike in Pride/Anger. Let *Desire* pay for the friction.
- **The One Ask:** One input field, one tap.
- **Optimization:** Frame it as a save-unlock: *"Enter your email so we can save your score and your free plan - so you don't lose it."* She is spending a tiny effort to protect something she already wants.

### 5 - Results: The Pain Beat (The Dip)

- **Her Rung (Entry):** Controlled return to day-one feeling
- **The One Job:** Provide radical validation. She must feel completely seen and reassured (*"I'm not crazy"*). This raises the stakes before the solution.
- **The One Ask:** Continue reading/scrolling.

> ⏱️ **The Transition Rule:** This dip must be short - two or three beats max, then climb immediately. If the pain beat lingers, she slides into **Apathy** (*"see, I'm broken, it's hopeless"*) and bounces. Touch it, don't dwell.
> 

### 6 - Results: The Lift

- **Her Rung (Entry):** Neutrality climbing to Desire
- **The One Job:** Fast emotional climb back up using a strict psychological sequence:
    1. **Score Card:** Brings clarity → **Neutrality**
    2. **"Why This Is Happening":** Blameless, mechanism-based reframe → Lifts her out of **Guilt**
    3. **"You're Not Alone":** Community data → Lifts **Grief** into belonging
    4. **Outcome Stat:** Re-lights **Desire**
- **The One Ask:** Move to diagnosis.

### 7 - Diagnosis

- **Her Rung (Entry):** Desire transitioning to **Reason**
- **The One Job:** Satisfy her logical mind and dismantle the scam objection before the final ask.
- **The One Ask:** Review the plan breakdown.
- **Sequence:**
    - Where this is heading *(light loss aversion)*
    - Before/after visual *(Desire)*
    - What you get back, using her exact words *(Desire Callback #1)*
    - Everything you unlock *(Reason: the plan/mechanism)*
    - **Trust Strip:** Place this *immediately* before the final ask. Make it heavy on clinical backing, Dr. Haver-style credibility, and transparent ingredients language.

### 8 - Paywall

- **Her Rung (Entry):** **Willingness / Reason** *(ready to decide at the top of the ladder)*
- **The One Job:** Close the loop using the same size of small, reversible step as the first click. Bookend the funnel.
- **The One Ask:** Start the trial.
- **Paywall Sequence:**
    1. **Desire Callback #2:** Connect directly to her named goal: *"{her goal} in 8 weeks"*
    2. **Reason/Logic Check:** Risk reversal via guarantee (*"score to 80+ or money back"*)
    3. **Courage-Sized Commitment:** Frame as a free start (*"free start, no charge for 3 days"*)

## 📈 High-Leverage Fixes (Ranked Priority)

1. **Sacred Goal Variable:** Turn the Goals quiz answer into a literal string variable that travels the entire funnel. Output it **verbatim** in the Offer Hook on the diagnosis screen and the paywall headline. Do not let it auto-paraphrase.
2. **Re-engineer the Email Gate:** Strip out any language that feels like a verification checkpoint. Rewrite it strictly as a save-your-progress benefit to bypass the scam filter. Keep it to one field, one button.
3. **Time-Box the Results Dip:** Enforce the emotional dip visually. Build the layout so the score card/lift sequence is visible within exactly one scroll of the initial pain headline.
4. **Escalate the Trust Strip:** Treat the credibility section as a hard requirement for the Reason stage, not footer decoration. Beef up the medical backing and ingredient transparency right above the CTA.
5. **The Operating Rule Verification:** Audit every single screen using the internal framework:
    - *What emotional rung does she enter this screen at?*
    - *Does this screen hand her exactly one clear step up?*

> **The Back-Half Rule:** The funnel touches her day-one emotion twice (results pain, paywall goal-callback), but **never asks her to decide from down there**. Every single CTA must fire from a rung above the line.
